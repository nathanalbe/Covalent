# Covalent
Covalent is an AI-powered margin intelligence platform for ecommerce brands.

Direct-to-consumer companies operate on thin margins and are constantly exposed to volatility from supplier costs, shipping rates, foreign exchange, and rising advertising costs. Most operators only realize there is a problem after their margins begin to collapse.

Covalent helps brands detect margin risk early.

By connecting to platforms like Shopify and ingesting operational and macroeconomic data, Covalent analyzes a company’s cost structure and performance metrics (such as ROAS, CAC, inventory turnover, and COGS) to estimate margin exposure.

The system generates a Margin Health Score, identifies the primary risk drivers affecting profitability, and recommends actions founders can take such as adjusting pricing, managing inventory, or reallocating marketing spend to protect cash flow.

## Core Features

### Shopify Integration
Sync order, product, and inventory data.

### Margin Health Score
Quantifies risk of margin compression.

### Macro Signal Monitoring
Tracks freight, inflation, FX, and other cost drivers.

### AI-Powered Insights
Explains risk factors and suggests corrective actions.

### Alerts & Recommendations
Proactive notifications when margin risk increases.

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind

- Backend: FastAPI (Python)

- Database: PostgreSQL

- Data Analysis: Pandas

- AI Layer: OpenAI API
- Deployment: Vercel + Render

## Vision

Covalent aims to become the financial intelligence layer for ecommerce brands, helping operators anticipate cost shocks, protect margins, and make better operational decisions in volatile markets.
